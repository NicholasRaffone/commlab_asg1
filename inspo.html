Meet the Cast

False Dichotomy

This film attempts to unearth the two extremes in media: the live, laugh, love joyful side, and the gritty, gruesome, ghastly serious side. Comparing famous media throughout the 20th and 21th century, Li and Raffone show how these two extremes don't full encapsulate the human condition, and we all live in a gray area inbetween.

I Have a Dream

Perhaps one of the most transformative speeches in history, Martin Luther King Jr speaks at Washington DC to the American people. Calling for a justice and an end to racism, this speech is unparalleled in its raw power.

The Bee Movie

Jerry Seinfeld has created another cult following with this hit 2007 animated film centered around pop-culture references, parody humor, and most importantly, bees. Social media hails this movie as one of the greats even without the prospects of a sequel.

We Shall Fight On The Beaches

Winston Churchill gave this speech to the UK House of Commons in 1940, addressing the state of the Second World War. This powerful speech gave confidence to the Allies about an eventual victory and is remembered to this day for Churchill's perserverance.

Darwinian M&M's

Who doesn't like M&M's? This piece's history is shrouded in mystery, as we know little to nothing about the genetic research being conducted by this internet user. We can only imagine and hope that the output of this research serves the greater good of this world.

Barack Obama at the DNC

Barack Obama in his speech at the Democratic National Convention in 2004 hits on many topics that still prevail to this day. He speaks of an America where pundits attempt to divide the great nation for their own gain, and hopes for a more united America.

The Japanese Heart Surgeon

The story of Hidetoshi Hasegawa, a heart surgeon turned warehouse worker who threw away years of his life to serve the Japanese people by sabotaging a crime lord. His tale has a happy ending, as he finds joy in his new life in the United States.
