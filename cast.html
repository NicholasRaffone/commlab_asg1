Meet the Cast

Nicholas Raffone

Why does everything have to be so serious? Why can't we just do and say lighthearted things? Raffone takes on the role of Thalia, the Muse of comedy in this film, tackling topics such as Bee politics, candy eugenics, and medical malpractice.

Nathan Li

The world is a harsh place. Not everything is sunshines and rainbows, action has to be taken to ensure the wellbeing of all. Li represented Melpomene, the Muse of tragedy, reenacting speeches from eras of war, civil unrest, and political divisiveness
